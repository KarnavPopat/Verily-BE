Who Are We?

Verily is a blogging website project by Karnav Popat. It aims to create a flexible and extensible template system to easily create blog sites from a variety of themes and designs. It's built using React, JQuery and vanilla Javascript to maximize both simplicity and modifiability. Verily allows you to generate your entire website with minimal input required, from a simple article document.

About Verity Today

Given the coming times, the circulation of print media has sadly gone down immensely, but it remains more vital than ever to stay updated. Verity wishes to provide this basic information to our readers in a way that interests them.

We hope to make this a very interactive forum for everyone by asking for inputs and suggestions from our readers so that you too, can have something which in a way is yours too. One main motive/slogan is "a little something for everyone" . We want our newsletter to have something of interest to everyone, thus bringing us to our wide range of departments.

Not only does Verity provide information and regular news updates on important subjects like global affairs and business, we also have departments like entertainment and creativity for you to enjoy as well as to support growing artists.
